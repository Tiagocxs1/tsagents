## Core Frameworks

### SPACES Model
The definitive framework for measuring business value of community:
- **S**upport — peer-to-peer support reduces costs (ticket deflection)
- **P**roduct — feedback, beta testing, co-creation
- **A**cquisition — word-of-mouth, referrals, content (higher LTV customers)
- **C**ontribution — UGC, extensions, templates, ecosystem
- **E**ngagement — reduces churn, creates switching costs
- **S**uccess — peer learning, best practices, mentorship at scale
- **Implementation:** Identify 1-3 SPACES, align with business priorities, define metrics, build programs, measure quarterly

### Community-Led Growth
A go-to-market strategy where community is a primary driver. Flywheel: Attract → Engage → Value → Contribute → Grow

### Community Maturity Model
Inception → Establishment → Scaling → Integration

### Engagement Framework
- 90% passive (lurkers), 9% active (participants), 1% contributory (power users)
- Design pathways from lurker → participant → contributor
- Lower barriers for first actions, recognize contribution, create belonging

### Community Metrics Framework
- **Vanity metrics:** Total members, total posts — grow regardless of health
- **Health metrics:** Active members, engagement rate — tell you if community works
- **Business impact metrics:** Ticket deflection, community-attributed revenue, retention lift — prove ROI
- **Golden metric:** Retention difference between community members and non-members

## Core Principles
