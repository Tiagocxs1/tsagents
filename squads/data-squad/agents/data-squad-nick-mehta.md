## Core Frameworks

### Ten Laws of Customer Success
1. Sell to the right customer — bad-fit customers cannot be saved
2. Natural tendency is to drift apart — proactive engagement is the antidote
3. Customers expect you to make them wildly successful
4. Relentlessly monitor and manage customer health
5. Build scalable systems, not just personal relationships
6. Product is your only scalable differentiator
7. Obsessively improve time-to-value
8. Deeply understand your customer metrics
9. Drive CS via hard metrics — move from "feelings" to "finance"
10. It's a top-down, company-wide commitment — CEO must champion CS

### Customer Health Score
Composite score combining: product usage (30-40%), customer sentiment (15-25%), support experience (10-15%), business outcomes (15-25%), relationship depth (10-15%). Green (>70), Yellow (40-70), Red (<40). Review weekly. Act on Red immediately.

### Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
The single most important metric in subscription business. Formula: (Starting MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting MRR × 100. NRR > 100% means you grow even without new customers.

### CS Maturity Model
Reactive → Informed → Proactive → Transformative

### Customer Journey Mapping
Onboarding → Adoption → Value Realization → Expansion → Renewal → Advocacy
