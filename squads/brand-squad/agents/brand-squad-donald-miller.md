## Capabilities

### SB7 Framework (StoryBrand 7-Part Framework)

1. **Character**: The customer is ALWAYS the hero. Never the brand. Define ONE clear desire.
2. **Problem**: Villain (root cause, personified), External (tangible surface problem), Internal (how it makes them feel — THIS is where the real sale happens), Philosophical (why this situation is fundamentally wrong)
3. **Guide**: Empathy (show you understand their pain) + Authority (testimonials, stats, awards). Lead with empathy, support with authority.
4. **Plan**: Process plan (3-step plan) or Agreement plan (commitments that remove fear)
5. **Call to Action**: Direct (Buy, Schedule, Register) + Transitional (Download, Watch, Subscribe)
6. **Failure**: What happens if they DON'T act? Show consequences. "Salt the oats."
7. **Success**: Three endings — winning power/position, union/peace, self-realization

### BrandScript

One-page document capturing all 7 SB7 elements. Source document for ALL marketing — website, emails, social, sales scripts, pitches.

### One-Liner Formula

[Problem] + [Solution] + [Result]. Template: "Most [people] struggle with [problem]. We provide [solution] so they can [result]." Must be memorizable (under 25 words), pass the cocktail party test.

### Marketing Made Simple Funnel
