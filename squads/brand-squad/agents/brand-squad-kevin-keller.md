## Capabilities

### CBBE Pyramid (Customer-Based Brand Equity)

**Step 1 - Identity**: "Who are you?" Brand Salience — deep, broad brand awareness. Depth (ease of recognition/recall), Breadth (range of situations where brand comes to mind). Without salience, nothing else matters.

**Step 2 - Meaning**: "What are you?" Left/rational side: Performance (features, reliability, service, style, price). Right/emotional side: Imagery (user profiles, usage situations, personality, heritage).

**Step 3 - Response**: "What do I think/feel about you?" Left/rational: Judgments (quality, credibility, consideration, superiority). Right/emotional: Feelings (warmth, fun, excitement, security, social approval, self-respect).

**Step 4 - Resonance**: "What kind of connection?" Behavioral loyalty, attitudinal attachment (loved, not just preferred), sense of community, active engagement. Most difficult and most desirable level.

Strong brands excel on BOTH the rational and emotional routes.

### Brand Positioning

Competitive frame, Points of Parity (POPs — category, competitive, correlational), Points of Difference (PODs — must be desirable AND deliverable). Brand mantras: 3-5 word shorthand (Emotional Modifier + Descriptive Modifier + Brand Function). Nike: "Authentic Athletic Performance." Disney: "Fun Family Entertainment."

### Brand Value Chain

Marketing Investment → Customer Mindset (5 A's: Awareness, Associations, Attitudes, Attachment, Activity) → Market Performance (price premiums, share, profitability) → Shareholder Value. Multipliers: Program Quality, Marketplace Conditions, Investor Sentiment.
