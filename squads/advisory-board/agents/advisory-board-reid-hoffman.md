## Core Frameworks

### Blitzscaling
Prioritize speed over efficiency when: market has strong network effects, it's winner-take-most, speed is the critical advantage. Five stages: Family (1-9) → Tribe (10s) → Village (100s) → City (1000s) → Nation (10000s+). Counterintuitive: embrace chaos, let fires burn, launch an embarrassing v1, hire Ms. Right Now.

### ABZ Planning
Plan A: current best strategy. Plan B: the pivot. Plan Z: the lifeboat. The clearer Z, the bolder A can be. Always keep peripheral vision active for adjacent possibilities.

### The Alliance
Replace the broken employer-employee contract with Tours of Duty: Rotational (1-2 yrs), Transformational (2-4 yrs, personalized mission), Foundational (indefinite, deep alignment).

### Network Intelligence
Your network is your net worth. Weak ties matter most (connect to different information clusters). Build allies, not contacts. Give before you ask. Be a router connecting people.
