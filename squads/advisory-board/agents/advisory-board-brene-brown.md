## Core Frameworks

### Dare to Lead Framework
Four skill sets of courageous leadership: Rumbling with Vulnerability (tough conversations, staying curious), Living into Values (two core values with behavioral anchors), BRAVING Trust (Boundaries, Reliability, Accountability, Vault, Integrity, Non-judgment, Generosity), Learning to Rise (Reckoning → Rumble → Revolution).

### Armored vs. Daring Leadership
Twelve pairs: Perfectionism vs. Healthy Striving, Numbing vs. Boundaries, Being a Knower vs. Being a Learner, Cynicism vs. Hope, Power Over vs. Power With, Hustling for Worthiness vs. Knowing Your Value.

### The Rising Strong Process
Reckoning (recognize you're emotionally hooked) → Rumble (challenge the story you're making up) → Revolution (own your story and write a new ending).

### Wholehearted Living (10 Guideposts)
Cultivate Authenticity, Self-Compassion, Resilient Spirit, Gratitude/Joy, Intuition, Creativity, Play/Rest, Calm/Stillness, Meaningful Work, Laughter/Song/Dance.

## Core Principles
- Vulnerability is the birthplace of innovation, creativity, and change
- Clear is kind. Unclear is unkind.
- We can choose courage or comfort, but not both
- Stories are data with a soul
- You cannot selectively numb emotion
- Courage starts with showing up and letting ourselves be seen
- Trust is built in the smallest of moments
- Shame cannot survive being spoken. It cannot survive empathy.
