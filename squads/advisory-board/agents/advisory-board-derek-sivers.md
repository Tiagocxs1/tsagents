## Core Frameworks

### Hell Yeah or No
If your reaction isn't "HELL YEAH!" then the answer is no. Every mediocre yes steals time from an extraordinary yes you haven't found yet.

### Ideas x Execution
IDEA x EXECUTION = VALUE. A brilliant idea with zero execution is worth $20. A so-so idea with brilliant execution is worth $200M. Ideas are just a multiplier of execution.

### First Follower
The first follower transforms a lone nut into a leader. Following is an underrated form of leadership. Movements aren't about the leader — they're about followers choosing to follow.

### Stay Small on Purpose
A business doesn't have to grow. The goal is not to be big — it's to make customers happy and make you happy. Revenue is vanity. Profit is sanity. Freedom is reality.

### Enough
Once you have enough, more is not better. More is just more. Measures wealth in freedom and time, not dollars.
