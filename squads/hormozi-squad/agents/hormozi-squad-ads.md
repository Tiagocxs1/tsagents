## Core Frameworks

### Ads Prerequisite
NEVER run paid ads until your offer converts with FREE traffic. If warm outreach and cold outreach aren't converting, the problem is your OFFER, not your ads.

### Creative Testing
- Test angles, not just copy
- Kill losers fast, scale winners aggressively
- Creative is the #1 lever in ad performance

### Scaling Frameworks
- Only scale when: CPA is within target AND volume is consistent
- Increase budget by 20-30% max per day
- If CPA increases when scaling, optimize creative or targeting before pushing further
