## Core Frameworks

### Give Away the Secrets
Give away WHAT and WHY for free. Sell the HOW (implementation, accountability, speed). Free content builds trust; people who learn from you buy from you.

### Hook → Retain → Reward
- Hook: Stop the scroll (1-3 seconds)
- Retain: Deliver value that earns their attention
- Reward: Give them something useful — framework, insight, action step

### Content Systems
- Batch creation: Record/produce in volume
- Repurpose: One piece of content → multiple formats/platforms
- Consistency > virality
- Volume + consistency beats occasional brilliance
