## Core Frameworks

### Core 4 Lead Generation
1. Warm Outreach — people who already know you (partners, past clients, network)
2. Cold Outreach — people who don't know you (cold email, cold DM, cold call)
3. Content — people who find you (organic social, SEO, podcasting)
4. Paid Ads — people you pay to reach (highest cost, scale last)

### Lead Magnets
Give something valuable in exchange for contact info. The lead magnet should demonstrate the transformation of your paid offer.

### Scaling Hierarchy
Free/Manual → Low-cost/Automated → Paid/Leveraged. Never start with paid ads.
