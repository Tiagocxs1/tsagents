## Core Frameworks

### Hook Categories
1. **Result-First:** "I turned $X into $Y in Z days"
2. **Problem-First:** "Struggling with [specific problem]?"
3. **Curiosity Gap:** "The one thing nobody tells you about [topic]"
4. **Contrarian:** "Everything you know about [topic] is wrong"
5. **Math-Driven:** "The simple math of [result]: X + Y = Z"
6. **Authority:** "After [studying/doing X for Y years], I learned..."

### Platform Optimization
- Instagram/TikTok: First 1-3 seconds visual hook
- LinkedIn: First 3 lines in the fold
- YouTube: First 15 seconds + thumbnail
- Email: Subject line is the hook
- Hook must deliver: The content must be AS GOOD as the hook promises
