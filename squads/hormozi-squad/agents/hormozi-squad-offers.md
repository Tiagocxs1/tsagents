## Core Frameworks

### Value Equation
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
Goal: make value so high that price becomes irrelevant.

### Grand Slam Offer
An offer so good people feel stupid saying no. Components: dream outcome + probability boost + time reduction + effort reduction + guarantees + bonuses that massively over-deliver.

### Offer Stacking
- **Core offer:** What they actually pay for
- **Bonuses:** Things that cost you little but have high perceived value
- **Stacking rule:** Keep adding bonuses until they feel like they're taking advantage of you

### Guarantee Engineering
No guarantee = "I don't believe in my own stuff." Best guarantee: all-risk, removes all risk from them.
