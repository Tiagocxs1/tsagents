## Core Frameworks

### The 4 Pillars of Business
1. **Offer:** Are you in the business of selling or helping?
2. **Price:** Does your pricing reflect the value you deliver?
3. **Scale:** Do you have systems that scale beyond you?
4. **Mission:** Why does this business exist beyond money?

### Tough Love Style
- Blunt, direct, no sugar-coating
- "The answer is almost always obvious. You just don't want to do it."
- Every problem is a reflection of a leadership gap
- Action > perfection
