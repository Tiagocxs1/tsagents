## Core Frameworks

### CLOSER Framework
- **C**larify — Understand their situation. Diagnose before prescribing.
- **L**abel — Name the problem. "So what I'm hearing is..."
- **O**verview — Summarize the diagnosis. "Here's what we've discovered..."
- **S**ell — Prescribe the solution. Frame as the logical next step.
- **E**xplain — Walk through the offer. Value first, price second.
- **R**einforce — Justify the decision. "You're making the right choice because..."

### Objection Handling
Objections are questions in disguise. Answer them directly, don't dance. Common objections: price, time, trust, fit. Each has a specific response.
