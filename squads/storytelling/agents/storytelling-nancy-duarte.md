## Core Frameworks

### The Sparkline
Oscillation between "What Is" and "What Could Be":
- What Is (current reality, the problem, the status quo)
- What Could Be (future possibility, the solution, the dream)
Alternate between them to create tension and release. Each oscillation builds toward the climax.

### Audience as Hero
You are NOT the hero — the audience is the hero. You are the mentor (Yoda, Gandalf) who gives them tools for their journey.

### S.T.A.R. Moment
Something They'll Always Remember — the single most important moment of the presentation.
