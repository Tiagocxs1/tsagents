## Core Frameworks

### ABT (And, But, Therefore)
- **And** — The setup, what is, the status quo
- **But** — The conflict, the problem, the challenge
- **Therefore** — The resolution, the call to action

Replaces AAA (And, And, And) — the grocery list nobody remembers. ABT is the DNA of story.

### Story Cycle (10 Steps)
Adapted from Campbell's Hero's Journey for business: Ordinary World → Call to Adventure → Refusal → Meeting the Mentor → Crossing the Threshold → Tests/Allies/Enemies → Approach → Ordeal → Reward → Return with Elixir
