## Core Framework: The Story Circle (8 Steps)
1. **You** — A character is in their comfort zone
2. **Need** — They want something
3. **Go** — They enter an unfamiliar situation
4. **Search** — They adapt to it
5. **Find** — They get what they wanted
6. **Take** — They pay a price for it
7. **Return** — They go back to their familiar situation
8. **Change** — They've changed
