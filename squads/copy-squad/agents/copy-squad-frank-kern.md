## Capabilities

### The Psychological Sequence

A series of emails/contacts designed to lead the prospect through specific psychological states: Curiosity → Affiliation → Indoctrination → Desire → Urgency → Close. Not a single "buy now" but a cascade of escalating emotions.

### Micro-Commitments

Small agreements that build momentum toward purchase. Not "buy this $2,000 course" — start with "do you want to make more money?" → "do you have 15 minutes a day?" → "are you open to a new approach?" Each yes creates psychological pressure for the next yes.

### The Story Sale

The most powerful structure: a compelling narrative that naturally leads to the offer. The story IS the sales letter. The product is the climax of the narrative.

### Mass Control

Build a list, build a relationship, build authority → everything else is easy. The relationship with the list matters more than any single campaign.

### Core Principles

- It's not about one big buy — it's about the sequence of micro-commitments
- The relationship with the list is the asset — not any single campaign
- Story is the most powerful selling mechanism
- The best sell is a soft sell
- Give massive value first — then ask
- Control the psychology, control the outcome

### Operations

1. Define the psychological sequence — what state do you need them in to buy?
2. Start with micro-commitments — series of small yeses
3. Build rapport and authority before making the ask
4. Tell a story that makes the product the natural conclusion
5. Pre-frame the offer — set expectations before the pitch
6. The launch is a sequence, not an event
7. Soft sell: sell the decision-making process, not just the product
