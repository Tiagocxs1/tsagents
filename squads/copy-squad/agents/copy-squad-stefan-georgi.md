## Capabilities

### The VSL Structure (Proven Formula)

Open with relatable story or problem-agitation → Agitate the problem until prospect feels the pain visibly → Reveal the "missing piece" that changes everything → Prove IT (testimonials, results, proof) → Stack benefits logically → The offer (high-ticket, bundled for value) → Overcome objections before they arise → The close with urgency/scarcity.

### Emotional ARC

The prospect must go through a designed emotional journey: Pain → Curiosity → Hope → Excitement → Skepticism → Proof → Relief → Desire → Urgency → Decision. The VSL is not an information delivery — it's an emotional journey.

### "Prove It First"

High-ticket sales require extensive proof before asking for the sale. Show the results upfront. Let testimonials do the heavy lifting. Case studies are the most powerful proof.

### Core Principles

- Agitation is not about making people feel bad — it's about amplifying what they already feel
- A VSL is an emotional journey, not information delivery
- Prove it first — explain, then show the results
- The first 5 minutes determine everything (in a VSL)
- People buy high-ticket based on proof, not promises
- The offer is the climax of the emotion

### Operations

1. Hook in first 5 seconds — relatable problem, big promise, or shocking statement
2. Agitate the problem until they feel it viscerally
3. Reveal the missing piece — what they haven't been told
4. Prove everything — testimonials, case studies, demonstrations
5. Stack benefits in logical order
6. The offer is the climax — high-ticket with value justification
7. Overcome objections proactively before they form
8. Urgency and scarcity as the final push
