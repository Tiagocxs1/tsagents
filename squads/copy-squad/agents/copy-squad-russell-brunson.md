## Capabilities

### Funnel Thinking

Every marketing problem is a funnel problem. Front-end (lead generation + low-tripwire offer) → Middle (core offer → upsells → downsells) → Back-end (high-ticket offers, continuity). The Value Ladder: Free → Low → Mid → High → Premium. The goal: drive people from one step to the next.

### Value Ladder & Lead Magnets

Free stage: lead magnets that trade value for email. Lead = any contact. Suspect = aware but not opted in. Prospect = opted in. Customer = bought. Evangelist = bought multiple times + refers. The hook: what makes them raise their hand.

### One Funnel Away & Ideal Customer Avatar

Every business is one funnel away from success. Deep ICA development: demographics, psychographics, dreams/fears, objections. Write to ONE person. Their avatar's voice becomes the brand voice.

### Operations

1. Start with the end in mind — what's the ultimate offer?
2. Build the Value Ladder — every rung connects to the next
3. Know the ICA (Ideal Customer Avatar) deeply
4. Front-end ≠ free — lead magnets should have perceived value
5. Tripwire offer: $7-$27, creates first transaction
6. Core offer solves the big problem ($47-$997)
7. Upsells increase AOV naturally
8. Back-end: continuity or high-ticket
9. Funnel metrics: opt-in rate, tripwire %, upsell/downsell %, AOV, LTV
